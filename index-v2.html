The L2E Process

Three simple steps to precision etching.

1

Apply Resist

Apply L2E etch resist to your metal. Then use your laser to etch the design through the resist.

2

Chemical Etch

Place in the L2E machine. Ferric chloride spray removes unprotected metal, creating deep, precise etchings.

3

Strip & Finish

Remove resist in caustic soda. Your deep-etched piece is ready. Clean, professional, permanent.
