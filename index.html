The L2E Process

Three simple steps to deep chemical etching.

1

Apply Resist

Apply L2E etch resist to your metal using a spray gun or screen print. Then use your laser to etch the design.

2

Chemical Etch

Place your metal in the L2E machine. The chemical spray removes unprotected metal, creating deep, precise etchings.

3

Strip & Finish

Remove the resist in a simple caustic soda solution. Your deep-etched piece is ready.
